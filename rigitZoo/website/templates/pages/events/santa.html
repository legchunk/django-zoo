Arctic & Antarctic Facts

Penguins: Penguins are native to the Southern Hemisphere — most species live in and around Antarctica, adapted for swimming rather than flying.

Polar Bears: Polar bears live in the Arctic (North Pole region). They are powerful swimmers and rely on sea ice for hunting seals.
