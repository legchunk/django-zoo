About Our Zoo

A brief introduction to our mission, history, and the animals we care for.

Founded in 1985, our zoo began as a small rescue and has grown into a regional center for conservation and animal care. We provide species-appropriate habitats, daily enrichment, and individualized veterinary attention to ensure every animal thrives.

Working with universities and conservation organizations, we run breeding and reintroduction programs for at-risk local species, and our staff lead research projects that support habitat restoration and long-term population monitoring.

Through guided tours, school partnerships, and community outreach, we aim to inspire visitors to support wildlife stewardship and sustainable practices. Your visits and donations directly fund care, conservation, and education initiatives across the region.
